I am a Member of Technical Staff at Adobe Systems, India. I have been part a wonderful engineering team that works on the Adobe Media Optimizer (AMO) product. AMO helps to suggest the best possible combination of search, display and social ads under budget constraints. It also goes one step further and automates the digital advertising plans.

Prior to joining Adobe, I completed my M.Tech from the Department of Computer Science, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Delhi.

I was fortunate to be advised by Professor Sandeep Sen during my stay at IIT Delhi. My dissertation work involved analysis of Randomized Rounding based solutions for Integer Linear Programming. The thesis can be found here.


During my undergraduate studies at Bengal Engineering & Science University (IIEST), Shibpur, I worked on Digital Micro-fluidic biochips simulator project under supervision of Professor Pranab Roy and Professor Hafizur Rahaman. Our work focused on functional testing of customized droplet route paths in a biochip.


Apart from the fascinating realm of computer science, literary fictions and photography pique my interest in leisure time.
