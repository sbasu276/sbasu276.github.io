About

Dr. Soumen Basu is works in Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and Medical Image Analysis. He is particularly interested in learning with limited supervised data for safety-critical tasks. He completed his PhD at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi, where he pioneered AI-based models for detecting gallbladder cancer from ultrasound images. His innovative work has been published in top-tier conferences such as CVPR, MICCAI, and WACV as well as renowned journals like The Lancet Regional Health and Medical Image Analysis.

Dr. Basu is currently a Research Scientist at Meta New York, working in developing state-of-the-art Large Foundation Models for the Monetization teams. Prior to joining Meta, he was a Senior Chief Engineer at Samsung Research Bangalore, leading research in on-device Foundation Models for video and image-based segmentation. His previous experience includes roles at Adobe Systems and Penn State University. Dr. Basu has been awarded prestigious honors such as the Prime Minister's Research Fellowship and the Outstanding Teaching Assistant Award at IIT Delhi. He was also a recipient of the MICCAI STAR Award (2022).
